Live signals

Every watchlist stock evaluated against all 14 strategies. Start with the violet "Composite A+" filter — those are the highest-probability stacked setups. Fall back to Tier-1 chips (RSI(2), PEAD, Gap-and-Go) if no A+ today.
READ THIS
EOD signals only — Stooq returns daily bars, not intraday. The "armed" flag means the framework would place a buy stop for tomorrow's session. Real entries also require L4 spread/gap checks at fill time, the L2 regime to still be tradeable, the stock's sector to still be in the top 3, and no earnings inside the next 7 trading days. Verify earnings dates yourself before placing any order.
